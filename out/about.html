About

Coalition For Nutrition, Health, and Development.

CORD Consultancy is a private consultancy hub combining public health expertise with private agility — driving evidence-based impact across Ethiopia and beyond.

Overview

Independent. Senior. Locally rooted.

CORD Nutrition and Health Trading PLC — operating as CORD Consultancy — was re-registered in 2026 under Ethiopia's new trade laws and directives. We are a private limited company delivering integrated consultancy, training, nutrition and diet therapy, and capacity-building services across five core domains.

Our team brings together specialists in public health, nutrition, food science, reproductive health, and management — combining the rigor of evidence-based practice with the agility of the private sector. We are committed to women and youth empowerment, sustainable value chains, and market-driven health and nutrition solutions.

Our story

Building Coalition for Professional Excellence

CORD Consultancy is a purpose-driven organization that combines public health expertise with private-sector agility. Our team of experienced professionals specializes in nutrition, health, and development initiatives, taking a results-driven approach to every project.

We envision ourselves as a Center of Alliance and Coalition for Professional Excellence — a collaborative space where stakeholders and partners come together to advance meaningful outcomes in health, nutrition, and sustainable development.

  1. 2024

    Foundations

    CORD Consultancy traces its beginnings to 2024, founded on the conviction that health, nutrition, and development must be advanced together — not in silos.

  2. 2025

    Building the Coalition

    Early engagements across SRH, AYH, ECD, and nutrition initiatives demonstrated the value of an integrated, evidence-based approach in real Ethiopian contexts.

  3. 2026

    A reinvigorated identity

    Re-registered as CORD Nutrition and Health Trading PLC under Ethiopia's new trade laws, formalizing our role as a private consultancy hub.

  4. Today

    An international footprint

    Internationally accredited, member of the Global Nutrition Report, and active participant in Share-Net Netherlands — a credible voice on the global stage.

Our pillars

Four pillars guide everything we do.

These foundational principles shape our approach to health, nutrition, and development — ensuring holistic, sustainable impact.

1

Trust in the CORD of Connection

Just as the spinal cord connects our body and mind, we trust in the power of nutrition to connect us emotionally, spiritually, and physically. CORD Nutrition is committed to providing scientifically backed, holistic solutions that support enduring health across generations.

2

Emotional, Spiritual & Nutritional Integration

We recognize that true wellness transcends mere physical health. Our approach nurtures the emotional and spiritual health of each individual, understanding that these elements are inseparable from proper nutrition and overall well-being.

3

Interconnectedness of Life

At CORD Nutrition, we believe that health begins at the roots — our body's interconnected systems. Nutrition is the foundation of life itself, nurturing every aspect of our being to foster harmony and balance from conception to age.

4

Commitment to Development & Sustainability

We are dedicated to advancing human development through personalized, compassionate health solutions rooted in trust and interconnectedness. Our mission is to foster sustainable growth empowering individuals and communities to thrive.
